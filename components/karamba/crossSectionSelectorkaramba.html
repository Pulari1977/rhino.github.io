Cross Section Selector (karamba)

Component Index > Karamba > Cross Section > Cross Section Selector (karamba)

Lets you select cross sections by name or regular expression from a list of cross sections.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Elements|IdentifiersElems|IdsIdentifiers of beams of beams with identifiers to attach a cross section to.Goo
Name or list index of cross sectionName|IndNames of cross sections to select (e.g. 'HEA100') or indexes of cross sections in list. Cross section names are case-sensitive. Everything that comes after '#' is comment. The first cross section whose name matches is selected. Regular expressions start with '&'.Goo
Cross sectionsCroSecsInput list of cross sections for the selection. If nothing is given the default cross section table is used.CrossSection

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
Cross sectionCroSecCross sectionCrossSection
InfoInfoInformation regarding the selection of cross sections.String
